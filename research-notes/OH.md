## Verified requirements (12 state-sourced + federal-framework inheritance)

Provider full license (§4731.41 + telehealth rule "licensed in Ohio"); NO separate telehealth
registration (§4743.09); synchronous video / audio-only / asynchronous all permitted if the
in-person standard of care is met (OAC 4731-37-01 — audio-only only within a bona-fide visit);
telehealth consent must be documented (OAC 4731-37-01); in-person exam required before a
Schedule II CS to a new patient, with (E) exceptions (OAC 4731-11-09(D)); OARRS query mandatory
for opioids/benzodiazepines (§4731.055); nonresident TDDD license required (§4729.54); Category
III license = CS Sch I–V (§4729.54); pharmacist-in-charge "full and actual charge" (§4729.27);
USP 795/797/800 compounding adopted by rule (OAC 4729:5-8-04).
