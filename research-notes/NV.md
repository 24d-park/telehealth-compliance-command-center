## Verified requirements

Full NV license OR the NRS 630.261 **telemedicine license** for out-of-state physicians (629.515(1)); telehealth
covers video, **audio-only** (629.515(6)(e)(2), added by the 2025 amendment), and async store-and-forward;
relationship may be established by telehealth "when clinically appropriate" (629.515(4)); **MANDATORY PDMP** —
639.23507(1) practitioner "shall, before issuing an initial prescription for a controlled substance ... obtain
a patient utilization report" and review it, q90d thereafter; nonresident pharmacy licensure (639.2328);
biennial CS registration + PDMP-access proof (453.226); managing pharmacist (639.220); **Internet-pharmacy Board
CERTIFICATION** required (639.23288 — a real online-pharmacy gate); compounding adopts 2023 USP-NF incl. 795/797
(NAC 639.670, R053-24 eff. 11-15-2024).

## Notable

- NV is one of the states with a genuine mandatory PDMP-query (verb verified "shall obtain/review").
- NV has BOTH an out-of-state telemedicine license (telehealthReg) AND an internet-pharmacy certification —
  two real registration gates most states lack.
- Audio-only is now expressly permitted (2025 amendment).
