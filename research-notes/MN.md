## Verified requirements

Full MN license OR a genuine out-of-state interstate telehealth REGISTRATION ($100 initial + $75/yr,
§147.032); telehealth = real-time two-way audio+video; audio-only permitted for scheduled appointments
meeting standard of care (WITH a July 1, 2027 sunset); store-and-forward included (§62A.673); relationship
may be established via telehealth, no in-person mandate (§147.033); PMP access required before initial
Sch II-IV opiate Rx since Jan 1, 2021 (§152.126 subd.6(d)); out-of-state pharmacy dispensing to MN residents
needs a MN license (§151.19); CS Rx form requirements (§152.11); PIC definition (§151.01 subd.22).

## Notable

- MN has a real, current out-of-state interstate telehealth registration (§147.032) — one of the few states
  that offers a registration alternative to full licensure.
- Flagged: the former nonresident-pharmacy subdivision §151.19 subd.2 was REPEALED in 2013 and consolidated
  into subd.1 — the tracker cites the current subd.1, not the repealed subdivision.
